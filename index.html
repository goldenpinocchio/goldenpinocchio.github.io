About

ASCIIPEPE V2 keeps the turntable spin, but pulls the portrait back so the full face fits the screen more comfortably. The goal is a clearer read of the Pepe shape, eyes, mouth, and shading without the bust feeling oversized.

Quick controls

• Space pause / resume
• Use the speed slider to slow down or speed up the 360° spin
• Resize the window — the ASCII grid adapts responsively
