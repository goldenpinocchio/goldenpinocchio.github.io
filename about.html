Our Story

We believe every business deserves a website that actually works.

Founded in 2018, SellWebsites started with a simple frustration: too many businesses had beautiful websites that converted nobody. We set out to change that.

The Beginning

From a garage startup to a full studio

SellWebsites began when our founder, Alex Chen, was running a small SaaS startup. He spent $15,000 on a "premium" website that looked stunning but generated exactly zero leads.

He knew the problem wasn't the design — it was the approach. So he built his own site, focused entirely on conversion. It worked. His trial signups tripled within two months.

Friends started asking for help. Then friends of friends. By 2018, Alex had quit his job and started SellWebsites — a studio dedicated to one thing: building websites that actually sell.

What We Stand For

Three principles that guide every project we take on.

Conversion First

Beautiful is nice. Converting is necessary. Every decision we make starts with "will this help the client get more customers?"

True Partnership

We're not a vendor you hand a brief to. We become an extension of your team — invested in your success like you are.

Honest Timeline

We don't overpromise. If something takes 3 weeks, we say 3 weeks. You'll never be surprised by delays we "didn't see coming."

Our Journey

2018

The Garage Days

Started as a one-person shop. First project: a landing page for a local bakery. It doubled their online orders.

2019

Hired First Teammate

Too many projects, not enough hours. Brought on Maya to handle design while Alex focused on strategy and development.

2020

Weathered the Storm

Pandemic hit. We pivoted hard into e-commerce and launched 12 online stores in 6 months. Survival mode became growth mode.

2021

50 Sites Milestone

Hit our 50th launched website. Threw a small party. Started turning down projects that weren't the right fit.

2023

Expanded to SaaS

Launched our first full-scale SaaS application. James joined to lead development. Began taking on more complex projects.

2026

150+ Sites Later

Today we're a tight 4-person team who's helped businesses generate millions in new revenue. And we're just getting started.
